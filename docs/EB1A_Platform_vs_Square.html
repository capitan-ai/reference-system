Section C: Real Production Errors Discovered

On the first day of deployment, our platform identified the following issues in Zorina Nail Studio's data that Square's own analytics missed:

IssueDetailsImpact
Revenue inflation Square analytics reported $105,952.77 (Feb 2026). Actual revenue: $100,819.14. Difference: tips were included in "revenue" total. $5,133.63 (5.1% inflation). Tax and financial planning based on wrong numbers.
Missing payments 47 payment records were processed by Square but excluded from Square's analytics. Reason: payments lacked direct location_id; linked only via orders. Thousands of dollars invisible to the business owner.
Referral self-abuse Customers creating multiple accounts with different phone numbers to collect referral bonuses for themselves. 4-layer fraud detection caught these. $5,410 in fraudulent referral claims blocked.
Duplicate customer profiles Same customers with multiple Square profiles (different phone/email combinations). Inflated customer count, fragmented history. Inaccurate customer metrics. Cannot track true retention.
Mixed booking statuses Square reported "Appointments" mixing ACCEPTED, CANCELLED, and NO_SHOW together. Our platform separates: 1,025 accepted, 282 cancelled, 0 no-show. Business owner couldn't distinguish real appointments from cancellations.
Total value of errors discovered on Day 1: $10,543+ — Revenue correction ($5,133) + Fraud prevention ($5,410) + Missing payment recovery (47 records)
