## THE PROBLEM

Small service business owners (nail salons, spas) have booking systems and payment tools but cannot answer critical questions: why customers leave, which staff members underperform, where revenue leaks. Square, the dominant POS, provides ~5 basic metrics. Owners need 65+. No existing product bridges this gap.

## THE SOLUTION

A SaaS platform that connects to a salon's existing data and delivers AI-powered actionable recommendations — not dashboards, not charts, but specific instructions: "Contact these 12 clients this week or lose $4,800."

## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE (Patent-Protected)

Provisional patent drafted covering 8 subsystems, 40 claims:

1. **Snapshot Economics** — freezes price and commission at booking time (Square cannot do this)
2. **Referral Engine** — automated with 4-layer fraud prevention (Square has no referral program — merchants requesting since 2017)
3. **Booking-Payment Reconciliation** — links bookings to payments (Square's APIs have no linking mechanism)
4. **Customer Intelligence** — 65+ precomputed metrics vs. Square's ~5
5. **AI Business Analyst** — domain-specialized recommendations, not generic charts
6. **Communication Tracking** — admin performance analytics across SMS, phone, email
7. **Digital Wallet** — serverless Apple Wallet pass generation with real-time balance updates
8. **Standalone POS** — progressive replacement of Square entirely
