Revenue inflated by $5,133.63 Square analytics used total_money_amount (includes tips) instead of amount_money_amount (service revenue only) 5.1% revenue inflation. Reported $105,952.77 vs actual $100,819.14 Platform uses correct field. Tips tracked separately.
47 payments invisible Square's analytics view used INNER JOIN with locations. Payments without direct location_id were excluded. Incomplete revenue picture. Under-counting transactions. Platform uses UNION query to capture payments linked via orders table.
$5,410 referral fraud Customers created multiple accounts with different phone numbers to self-refer and collect bonuses. Direct financial loss through fraudulent reward payouts. 4-layer fraud detection: phone normalization, email matching, IP correlation, behavioral analysis.
Mixed booking statuses Square counted ALL booking statuses as "appointments" including cancelled and no-show. Inaccurate KPIs. Owner couldn't distinguish real vs cancelled. Platform filters: ACCEPTED only for KPI. Tracks cancelled (282) and no-show (0) separately.
